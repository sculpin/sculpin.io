Community

The Sculpin community is small but growing! Please join in!

Communication

Have questions or what to get involved? Here are the best ways to find us:

IRC

Join the #sculpin IRC channel on irc.freenode.org. This is where most of the action is currently!

Twitter

Mention @getsculpin on Twitter. This is a great way to call attention to yourself!

Mailing List

Join the Sculpin Users mailing list. Currently not super active; everyone seems to hang out in IRC instead!
